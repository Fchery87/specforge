# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 14, 2026

SpecForge ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our website and services.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1. Account Information

We use Clerk for authentication. When you sign up, we receive information such as your name, email address, and profile picture.

### 1.2. Project Content

We collect the project descriptions, questions, and answers you provide to generate project artifacts.

### 1.3. LLM Credentials

If you provide your own API keys for LLM providers (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic), these are encrypted and stored securely on our servers. They are decrypted only when used to make requests to the respective AI providers on your behalf.

### 1.4. Usage Data

We may collect information about how you interact with the Service, including IP addresses, browser types, and usage patterns, to improve our Service.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
- Process and generate your project artifacts.
- Secure your credentials and authenticate your access.
- Communicate with you about updates or support requests.

## 3. Sharing of Information

### 3.1. AI Providers

To generate project artifacts, we share your project-related Input Content with third-party LLM providers (including OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepSeek, Z.AI, and Minimax). These providers process data according to their own privacy policies. We do not share your account credentials with these providers.

### 3.2. Service Providers

We may share information with vendors who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting (Vercel) and database management (Convex).

### 3.3. Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

## 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption of sensitive data (like API keys) using `CONVEX_ENCRYPTION_KEY`, to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

## 5. Your Choices and Rights

You can access, update, or delete your account information through your settings. You can also delete your projects and associated artifacts at any time.

## 6. Retention of Data

We retain your information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page.

## 8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@specforge.ai.
